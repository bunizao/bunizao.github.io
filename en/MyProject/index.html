URLShortener

What can it do?

  • Shortens a very long link to the form of https://tuu.cat/xxxx.
    • Ideal for scenarios such as AFF where an elegant domain name is required.

Caution

  • xxxx is a random combination of letters and numbers, not necessarily 4 characters (there may be more).
  • In the spirit of freedom, we are open to custom short link suffixes, but be warned: Overly simple suffixes may invite scanning and blasting by cyber attackers with ulterior motives, and this site is not responsible for unnecessary risks to privacy.
    • Custom suffixes audit some offensive words, such as d*ck, f*ck, p**sy, sb, etc.
    • This site reserves the right to refuse the use of our services for spinach, pornography, scams and other illegal businesses.
    • We protect your privacy and guarantee that your original link will not be disclosed as long as you use it in a legal and reasonable manner.
  • This site uses Google’s reCAPTCHA authentication and may be slow to load on first visit.
  • We do not guarantee SLAs, but we will do our best to maintain availability.
  • ** We don’t charge, ever. **

SubConverter

What can it do?

  • Functionality: convert subscription links from different platforms to each other.
  • Security: We have a pure backend without any logs, your subscription links are stored very securely locally and do not pass through our servers, we guarantee that the process is completely private.
  • Feature: You can snare the TutuBetterRules shunt configuration in one click, this is probably the best shunt configuration for iOS & MacOS to use.
  • Similarly, this process does not go through our servers.
  • To avoid privacy risks, we removed the URLShortener integrated in the panel.

Caution

  • The front end as well as the back end 1 and back end 2 of this site are globally routed by Cloudflare(I love Cloudflare!). There may be special sanctions during special times, so availability in the continental direction is not guaranteed (but it is available during normal times).
    • We still recommend to use proxy access.
